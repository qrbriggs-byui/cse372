## ğŸ‘€ Step 2: Conduct the Observation
While the participant performs the task:
- Stay quiet and neutral. Blend in and let them work naturally.  
- Take **structured notes** that capture:  
  - **Steps:** What actions are performed and in what order?  
  - **Tools:** What systems, devices, or materials are used?  
  - **Challenges:** Where do delays or errors occur?  
  - **Workarounds:** What informal fixes or shortcuts are used?  
  - **Tacit insights:** What assumptions or habits are visible but unstated? (gestures, pauses, coordination cues, etc.)  

Use a simple format like this:

> **Task:** Booking a flight online  
> **Observation:** User opens three tabs to compare airlines.  
> **Challenge:** Confused by inconsistent baggage policies.  
> **Tacit insight:** User relies on color-coding flights by airline to track choices.
