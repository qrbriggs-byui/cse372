### **1.** What is the main goal of collaborative negotiation in software projects?

A. To ensure one party wins the negotiation
B. To find a middle ground that pleases the client only
C. To reach a shared agreement that benefits all parties
D. To avoid discussions about project requirements
✅ **Answer:** C

---

### **2.** How does collaborative negotiation differ from traditional bargaining?

A. It focuses on the “why” behind requests instead of dividing resources.
B. It aims to make compromises as quickly as possible.
C. It requires no discussion with stakeholders.
D. It’s primarily about lowering project costs.
✅ **Answer:** A

---

### **3.** In the e-commerce platform example, what solution represented collaborative negotiation?

A. Canceling the recommendation feature
B. Adding every feature marketing requested
C. Replacing the advanced engine with a simple “Customers also bought” section
D. Ignoring the marketing director’s request to save time

### **4.** What did the healthcare app example illustrate about negotiation?
