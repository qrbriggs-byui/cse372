Here are **10 multiple-choice questions** based on the attached reading, *How to Apply Prioritization Techniques (MoSCoW, WSJF) in an Agile Environment.*
Each question includes four possible answers (A–D) and the correct answer key.

---

### **1.** What is the main purpose of prioritization in Agile development?

A. To eliminate user stories that are not well defined
B. To determine the most valuable work to complete first
C. To ensure all team members have equal workloads
D. To finalize the product design before development begins
✅ **Answer:** B

---

### **2.** In Agile, what is the **Product Backlog**?

A. A static list of completed tasks
B. A record of rejected project ideas
C. A dynamic list of potential features, fixes, and improvements
D. A timeline for future sprint reviews
✅ **Answer:** C

---

### **3.** What are the four categories in the **MoSCoW** method?

A. Must have, Should have, Could have, Won’t have (for now)
B. Major, Secondary, Critical, Optional
C. Mandatory, Suggested, Conditional, Withdrawn
D. Minimal, Substantial, Complete, Waiting
✅ **Answer:** A

---

### **4.** Which of the following is a **“Must have”** feature in the MoSCoW method example for a food delivery app?

A. Real-time delivery tracking
B. Restaurant browsing and order placement
C. Driver rating system
D. Scheduled recurring orders
✅ **Answer:** B

---

### **5.** What does the **WSJF** acronym stand for?

A. Weighted Standard Job Factor
B. Work Scheduling for Job Forecasting
C. Weighted Shortest Job First
D. Workflow System Job Framework
✅ **Answer:** C

---

### **6.** In WSJF, what does the **Cost of Delay (CoD)** represent?

A. The total cost to complete the job
B. The time lost waiting for another team to finish
C. The impact or value lost if a feature is delayed
D. The estimated budget for each sprint
✅ **Answer:** C

---

### **7.** According to the WSJF formula, which feature should be completed first?

A. The feature with the highest Cost of Delay, regardless of effort
B. The feature with the smallest Job Size
C. The feature with the highest WSJF score (Cost of Delay ÷ Job Size)
D. The feature that is easiest for the team to develop
✅ **Answer:** C

---

### **8.** When is it best to use the **MoSCoW** method?

A. When the backlog contains measurable value and effort data
B. During early project stages, when priorities are qualitative
C. After all sprints have been completed
D. Only for large-scale enterprise projects
✅ **Answer:** B

---

### **9.** What is one key tip for successful prioritization in Agile?

A. Revisit priorities regularly, since value may change over time
B. Keep prioritization discussions limited to the Product Owner
C. Avoid documenting trade-offs to save time
D. Focus solely on technical feasibility, not user value
✅ **Answer:** A

---

### **10.** According to the reading, what is the ultimate goal of using techniques like MoSCoW and WSJF?

A. To complete every feature on the backlog
B. To help teams make smart, value-driven decisions together
C. To replace stakeholder feedback with data-driven scoring
D. To ensure faster development regardless of value
✅ **Answer:** B
