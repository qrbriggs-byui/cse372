# Quiz 2-4: How to Perform a Competitive Analysis Informed by Real User Insights

## Question 1
**What makes a competitive analysis more meaningful according to the reading?**
- A. Comparing as many competitors as possible
- B. Using user research to focus on what matters most to users
- C. Evaluating visual design only
- D. Ignoring emotional aspects of UX  
✅ **Correct Answer:** B

---

## Question 2
**What is the primary purpose of a competitive analysis?**
- A. To copy the best features from competitors
- B. To understand how others solve similar problems and identify opportunities
- C. To determine which company has the biggest market share
- D. To rank competitors by popularity  
✅ **Correct Answer:** B

---

## Question 3
**Which of the following describes an indirect competitor?**
- A. A product that serves the same audience with identical features
- B. A product that solves the same problem in a different way
- C. A product that is unrelated but popular
- D. A company that offers technical support services  
✅ **Correct Answer:** B

---

## Question 4
**Why is it important to gather user insights before conducting a competitive analysis?**
- A. It ensures the analysis focuses on what users truly care about
- B. It helps identify which competitors to avoid researching
- C. It replaces the need to test competitor products
- D. It guarantees faster analysis results  
✅ **Correct Answer:** A

---

## Question 5
**What should you include in a feature comparison matrix?**
- A. All possible features competitors have
- B. Only the features revealed as important during user research
- C. Randomly selected technical specifications
- D. Marketing slogans of competitors  
✅ **Correct Answer:** B

---

## Question 6
**What does the reading suggest evaluating besides functional features?**
- A. Team size and company history
- B. The user experience and emotional tone
- C. The competitor’s marketing budget
- D. The speed of their release cycle  
✅ **Correct Answer:** B

---

## Question 7
**When analyzing competitors’ UX, which factor is most important to note?**
- A. Whether users feel encouraged, overwhelmed, or indifferent while using it
- B. The company’s social media presence
- C. The number of screens in the app
- D. The pricing structure  
✅ **Correct Answer:** A

---

## Question 8
**What is the goal of identifying gaps and opportunities?**
- A. To mimic competitors’ designs more accurately
- B. To uncover unmet user needs and areas for innovation
- C. To focus on cost-cutting opportunities
- D. To find marketing partnerships  
✅ **Correct Answer:** B

---

## Question 9
**What is a recommended way to share your competitive analysis findings?**
- A. A dense written report with no visuals
- B. A short, visual presentation with quotes and actionable insights
- C. A list of copied competitor features
- D. A spreadsheet of feature counts only

## Question 10
**According to the reading, what is the ultimate goal of competitive analysis?**
